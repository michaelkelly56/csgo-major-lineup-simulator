## Overview

CS:GO Major Draft Simulator is a browser-based tool for draft planning and team construction with a major-style roster workflow. It is aimed at users who want to try out player picks, arrange lineups, and follow card-driven draft progression in one front-end experience.

Instead of leaning on heavy configuration, the simulator is built for quick scenario setup. It ships with region and era filtering, role-aware lineup placement, and major simulation logic, making it easy to model multiple combinations and review different draft results directly in the browser.
